About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the University of Birmingham working in both nuclear astrophysics and structure. A large area of my research uses the HF-ADNeF (High Flux Accelerator Driven Neutron Facility) to irradiate samples to study nuclear astrophysics. I also work with external users to allow them to access the facility through the NNUF.

Research Interests

Nuclear structure: clustering of light nuclei, near threshold-resonances, multibody decays

Nuclear astrophysics: understanding the gamma-ray signatures of stars, direct s/i-process studies

Time Projection Chambers: development of the next-generation TPC - TeBAT
